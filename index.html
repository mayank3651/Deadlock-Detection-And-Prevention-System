Conditions

  • Hold & Wait
    Process holds and requests more.
  • No Preemption
    Resource can't be forcibly taken.
  • Circular Wait
    Processes wait in cycle.

Solutions

  • Request all at once or release held.
  • Allow preemption based on priority.
  • Global ordering of resource access.
